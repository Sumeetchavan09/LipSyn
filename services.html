What We Offer

Face detection: It locates human faces in images/videos, using algorithms to extract features, applied in security, biometrics, and entertainment. e.g., face unlock on smartphones, emotion recognition, virtual reality, augmented reality, and social media filters.
Neural Machine Translation (NMT) :The NMT module is employed to translate the transcribed text into any regional language.
Lip reading: It also known as speechreading, involves understanding speech by visually interpreting the movements of the lips, face, and other facial cues.
LipGAN Visual Module: The LipGAN visual module is a novel component of the project that generates realistic talking faces from the translated audio of the input video.
