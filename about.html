Our Story

1.Input English Video- Obtain video content for dubbing.
2.Text Extraction- Use Assembly AI to extract text from the English video.
3.Translation- Utilize a Neural Machine Translation (NMT) model to translate the extracted English text into Kannada.
4.Input Transcribed Audio- Employ a External Audio File to generate speech from the translated KannadaAudio.
5.Output Dubbed Video- Combine the translated speech with the original video to create a dubbed video with lip synchronization.
6.Evaluation- Conduct experiments and surveys to assess the effetiveness of the automated dubbing system.
7.Results Analysis- Analyze the results obtained from experiments and surveys.
8.Iterate or Finalize- Based on the analysis, iterate the system for improvements or finalize the automated dubbing process
